Royal College

About

Royal College is a selective entry boys school located in Colombo, SriLanka. It started as a private school by Rev Joseph Marsh in 1835. It was established as the Colombo Academy by Sir Robert Wilmot-Horton. in January 1836, as part of the implementation of the recommendations of the Colebrooke Cameron Commission (1833), and was the first government run secondary school's for boys in the island.
